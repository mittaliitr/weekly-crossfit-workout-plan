Weekly CrossFit Workout Plan

Monday: EMOM (Every Minute on the Minute) - 20 minutes

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching

Workout:

  • Minute 1: 10 Push-ups
  • Minute 2: 15 Air Squats
  • Minute 3: 10 Dumbbell Rows (each arm)
  • Minute 4: 15 Sit-ups

Repeat for 5 rounds

Tuesday: AMRAP (As Many Rounds As Possible) - 20 minutes

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching

Workout:

  • 10 Dumbbell Thrusters
  • 15 Kettlebell Swings
  • 20 Box Jumps (or step-ups)
  • 200-meter Run (or 1 minute of high knees)

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes

Wednesday: Strength & Skill

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching

Workout:

  • Deadlift: 5 sets of 5 reps
  • Pull-ups: 5 sets of 5 reps (use bands or assisted pull-up machine if needed)
  • Plank Holds: 3 sets of 1 minute

Thursday: Active Recovery/Stretching

Activities:

  • Light yoga or stretching routine (20-30 minutes)
  • Leisure walk with your baby (30-45 minutes)

Friday: For Time

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching

Workout: 21-15-9 reps of:

  • Burpees
  • Dumbbell Snatches (alternating arms)
  • Wall Balls (or medicine ball throws)

Complete the workout as quickly as possible

Saturday: Chipper

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching

Workout:

  • 50 Jump Rope (or jumping jacks)
  • 40 Lunges (alternating legs)
  • 30 Push Press (with dumbbells)
  • 20 Sit-ups
  • 10 Burpees

Complete the sequence in the shortest time possible

Sunday: Rest or Light Activity

Activities:

  • Leisure walk with your baby
  • Gentle stretching or yoga
